PwllHeli Sailing Club

A specialised App to control the interior and exterior feature lights of a sailing club. The App uses colour and wind to create ambient light displays that inform the sailors of wind direction and force.

My Role

To design wind to light experiences, user experience, visual design (branding) and App icon. I worked closely with client and programmers to ensure the final app and light experiences looked good and worked well in it’s environment. The App is currently installed in PwllHeli Sailing Club.

Tools and Technologies

Pen and Paper, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop.

Mobile Platforms

PC and mobile (Android)
