Urban Fitness

The App controls dynamic light-scenes that adapt to the multi-use of workout spaces in gyms. The Gym lighting can be managed by building managers and gym instructors to set up and encourage different exercises.

My Role

To design the user experience, visual design (branding) and App icon for mobile and PC applications. I worked closely with the programmers to make sure the final app looked and worked as well as it should. The app is currently installed in Urban Fitness Gyms.

Tools and Technologies

Pen and Paper, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Indesign, Axure.

Zone Control

Live status notifications in each zone informs the user of zone lighting and zone occupancy. Users can navigate between zones easily and access zone lighting controls from anywhere in the building.

Studio Menu

Each zone has a selection of light-scenes that enhance the use and user experience within a zone.

Light-Scenes

Light-scenes can be tailored for an immersive work-out experience. Alternatively, some pre-programed light-scenes sense and adapt with the environment.
