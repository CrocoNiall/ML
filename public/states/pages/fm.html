Molex Tool Suite:
Facility Manager

The App supports Facility Managers to manage and control lights in a building. It provides a helicopter view of an entire building and individual local configuration and adjustment. The interface makes the building management process more straightforward and secure, allowing instant override or troubleshooting.

My Role

I designed the user experience, visual design (branding), app icon and developed new tools and light experiences to support Facility Managers and building management. The app is currently installed in Chicago. The projects success has developed the work relationships with Molex.

Builing Overview

Overview of building information, zone summary and error notifications with navigation links to zones and more information.

Building Layer

The building layer ensures light to the whole building within a time period allocated. The lights change behaviour according to the working hours of the zone to support users during work and save energy out of hours.

Zone Overview

Live zone status with regards to zone availability, occupancy and light status with links to individual zones and their lighting controls.

Zone Control

Zone control allows management of user zones and Light-Scene experiences. The Facility Manager has priority control at all times and can override, adjust and change live settings.

Zone Menu

The zone control menu provides management of user Light-Scenes, mood settings, sensor input, idle zone behaviour, light maintenance and energy management.

Building Zones

Building zones are categories that organise areas of a building into their use and function. Each building zone has set light-scenes and controls to manage the space.

Building Zone

The interface provides immediate access and control for any zone, allowing instant override or troubleshooting.
