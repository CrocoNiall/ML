Visualisation and Graphics

For the T32 Japanese sports car I designed the interior dashboard, graphics and car badge. Working in a small, dynamic visualisation team my contribution varied from exterior conceptual sketches, interior styling, model making and branding. I followed my designs through to manufacture and worked with external suppliers to ensure manufactured parts were exact to the engineering drawings.

Graphic T-shirt Range

I produced leading graphics used across global core clothing ranges and created entire ranges applying a style across independent graphics. I also managed the process from concept to manufacture including working with oversee factories and sample quality checks.

Press Ready

Meeting Banners

In a fast paced environment, I designed and printed meeting banners, for high level client meetings, that communicated project progress and described technology concepts to business men. The banners visualised work across departments and fit the length of the meeting room.

Halo Exterior Lighting

Halo is personal project of mine that I have designed, developed and hand made into a working prototype. It is an outdoor ambient lighting system for parks that displays immersive light experiences based on environmental factors like wind, temperature, light and presence. I am currently developing an App for user control.
