Molex Tool Suite: End User Tool

The App provides office workers personalised light control of work spaces in a building. Workers have dynamic light-scenes choices to support work tasks, mood and well-being. Light-scene availability varies in a building to enable task-specific lighting.

My Role

To lead user research and define user experiences, visual design (branding) and light experiences for workers. I conducted user trials to test new light authoring concepts and tools and communicated ideas in design documents and presentations for the client. The app is currently installed in Chicago. The success of the project has led to more work and user tool development.

Light-Scenes

Users can select a light-scene and personalise the settings to suit their work, mood and well-being. The simple design allows a quick control of the lights and room set up.

Moods

The colour and intensity of the lights can vary to support a users work activities and desired mood e.g. relaxation. No change is more than two selections away given an immediacy of response without complexity.

Personalise Circadian Lighting

Users can personalise Immersion and bio-adaptive levels in the environments for personal taste and better well-being. A reset button returns the light-scene to its default settings.

About

‘About’ informs the user of the Light-Scenes available to them, their description and icon identity. I designed the light-scene experiences, variable light effects and App icons for the App.

User Zones

Activity and context appropriate Light-Scenes are provided for different user zones in a building. In office buildings users have control of meeting zones, lab zones, desk zones and break-out zones.
