Bridgewater Hall

The App controls multiple spaces at Bridgewater Hall’s and provides audiences with a richer, immersive experience. The quick settings menu and intuitive light controls provide light technicians full control of a zone at the touch of a button.

My Role

To work with Bridgewater Halls lighting technicians and design the user experience, visual design and App icon. I ensured the final App looked good and worked well in it’s environment. The App is currently installed in Bridgewater Hall.

Quick Settings and Zone control

Lighting Technicians can control the lights from anywhere in the building, using mobile devices. The quick settings menu lists standard controls for room set ups. Zone control provides a greater degree of control available.

Zone control

Each zone has between 4 to 8 light-scenes that were specified and named by the client. The Light-scenes are pre-programmable, dynamic light displays at the touch of a button.

Front of House

Users have full control of Front of House. Each space has immersive lighting to enhance the visitors experience.

PC Application

Users can control the lights from a PC and mobile application.
